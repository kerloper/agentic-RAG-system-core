# Kerloper Finance - FAQ

## What is Kerloper?

Kerloper is a digital-first financial services company offering investment tools, portfolio analytics, and automated trading insights for retail and institutional clients.

## How do I open an account?

You can open an account by signing up on the Kerloper platform and completing KYC verification. Approval usually takes 1–2 business days.

## What services does Kerloper provide?

* Portfolio tracking and analytics
* AI-based investment recommendations
* Risk scoring for assets
* Automated trading signals

## Is my data secure?

Yes. Kerloper uses end-to-end encryption and complies with international financial data protection standards.

## Can I delete my account?

Yes. Users can request account deletion from the settings panel. Data retention follows regulatory compliance rules.
