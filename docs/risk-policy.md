# Kerloper Finance - Risk & Compliance Policy

## Risk Levels

Kerloper classifies all investment assets into 5 risk categories:

* Very Low Risk (Government bonds, stable assets)
* Low Risk (Blue-chip stocks, ETFs)
* Medium Risk (Balanced portfolios, index funds)
* High Risk (Growth stocks, crypto exposure)
* Very High Risk (Derivatives, leveraged assets)

## Risk Scoring System

Each user portfolio receives a dynamic risk score between 0 and 100 based on:

* Asset volatility
* Market exposure
* Liquidity risk
* Diversification level

## Compliance Rules

* Kerloper complies with AML (Anti-Money Laundering) regulations
* KYC is mandatory before trading
* Suspicious activity is flagged automatically

## Trading Restrictions

Certain regions may have restricted access due to regulatory constraints.
