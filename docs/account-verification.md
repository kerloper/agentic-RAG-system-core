## Required Verification Documents
Kerloper requires customers to provide identity documents for verification. Accepted documents include government-issued identification and proof of address. Both personal and business accounts must submit valid documentation. The documents are reviewed to ensure they meet verification standards.

## Verification Timeline
Verification typically takes one to three business days to complete. The review timeline depends on the volume of applications and the clarity of the submitted documents. Customers receive a notification once the verification process is finished. Expedited review may be available for certain account types.

## Verification Failure Process
If verification fails, Kerloper informs the customer with the reason for the failure. The customer can then correct or provide additional documentation. Failed verification remains unresolved until the required documents are successfully resubmitted. The account may remain restricted until verification is complete.

## Platform Access Before Verification
Customers may have limited access to the platform before verification is complete. Basic account registration is available, but full financial services require verification. This restriction helps protect both the customer and the platform. Access to trading, transfers, and portfolio features is enabled only after approval.

## Resubmitting Documents
Customers can resubmit documents through the verification section of the dashboard. The platform provides guidance on acceptable document formats and required information. It is important to address the issue noted in the verification failure. Once documents are resubmitted, the review process begins again.
